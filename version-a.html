Ideas

Short version

AI is going to change how people interact with computers. A lot of office work becomes managing agents instead of manually driving every app.

Edge AI matters. Devices keep getting better and models keep getting smaller and more efficient.

Multimodal agents are the future. Text-only chat is useful, but real work touches code, screenshots, logs, documents, video, audio, tools, and systems.

SaaS is going to get squeezed because agents will make custom internal software easier to build and maintain.

AI does not need to be sentient for the practical impact to be huge. Capability matters more than the semantics.
